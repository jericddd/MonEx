MonEx Documentation

Getting Started

Catch Monanimals using Monballs and add them to your party.

Battles

Select a Monanimal from your party to battle wild creatures and gain XP.

Skills & Rarity

Each Monanimal has unique skills. Higher rarity Mons have more skills and better stats.

Progression

Level up your Mons through battles and collect stronger creatures.

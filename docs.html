MonEx Documentation

Catch Monanimals on X, train your party, and battle through the Monad office. This page covers what you need to play — no dev setup required.

NEWBIE GUIDE

Step-by-step: login, catch on X, daily rewards, party, battles, and quests.

WHAT'S COMING

Arena betting with $MONEX, PVP Wager, Monball shop payments, and Marketplace.

Core gameplay

  • Catch on X — tweet @monexmonad catch N monanimals (1 Monball per catch). Mons sync to your Party, then Box.
  • Party — up to 3 fighting Mons. Box — storage for the rest.
  • Daily login — claim 5 Monballs from the home page; collect them in your in-game Mailbox.
  • Resource chest — AFK rewards on the main scene. Tap when READY.

Battles

  • Adventure → Campaign — chapter story, bosses, and stage progression.
  • Adventure → Patrol — scan the Monad office for gear drops (daily scan limit).
  • House Tower — climb floors for tougher fights and loot.
  • Arena — coming soon. Bet $MONEX on arena match outcomes. Details →

Progression

  • Shop — Monballs, materials, and items (Gold / KB's Onion). $MONEX Monball packs coming soon.
  • Armory — equip, enhance, and synthesize gear.
  • Quests — daily, weekly, and campaign tasks with milestone chest rewards.
  • Rarity — Common, Uncommon, Rare, Legendary, Mythic. Higher rarity = better stats and more skills.

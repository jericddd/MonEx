Newbie Guide

Everything you need to start playing MonEx — catch Monanimals on X, build your party, and battle through the Monad office.

1. Create your account

  1. Go to the MonEx home page.
  2. Click LOGIN WITH X and sign in with your X account.
  3. Click GAME START in the sidebar to enter the game.

Logging in with X saves your progress to the cloud and links your catches to your account.

2. Set up your X handle

  1. In the game, open PROFILE (left rail).
  2. On the ACCOUNT tab, enter your X @handle and tap SAVE.
  3. Use the same handle you post catches from on X.

Your handle connects wild catches on X to your in-game Party and Box.

3. Catch Monanimals on X

  1. On X, post a tweet like:
    @monexmonad catch 10 monanimals
  2. Each catch costs 1 Monball. Catching 10 Mons uses 10 Monballs.
  3. Wait a moment, then open PROFILE → CATCH LOG in the game to see your results.
  4. New Mons go to your Party first (up to 3 slots). When the party is full, extras go to your Box.
Tip: The home page X Wild Log shows live catches from everyone. Click a row to see the full catch breakdown with skills and rarity.

You can also tap CATCH ON X in the game for a quick reminder of the tweet format.

4. Claim your daily reward

  1. On the home page, tap the DAILY LOGIN box (right sidebar).
  2. You receive 1 Monball sent to your in-game MAILBOX.
  3. Open the game → MAILBOX (left rail) → claim the reward.

Daily login has a cooldown — come back after the timer resets for your next Monball.

5. Know your Party and Box

  • Party — up to 3 Monanimals that fight for you. Open PARTY on the right rail to view or swap members.
  • Box — storage for extra Mons. Move Mons between Party and Box anytime.
  • Each species can only appear once in your party at a time.

6. Battle and progress

Main scene (home base)

Your party idles on the main scene. Tap the resource chest when it shows READY to collect AFK rewards (gold, trainer XP, and more).

Battle Hub

Open BATTLE on the right rail to enter the Battle Hub. From there:

  • Adventure — choose Campaign (story chapters and bosses) or Patrol (office scans for gear and loot). Patrol has a daily scan limit.
  • House Tower — climb tower floors for tougher fights and rewards.
  • Arena and PVP Wager — coming soon. See What's Coming for the Arena betting plan.

7. Grow stronger

  • Shop — buy Monballs, materials, and items with Gold or KB's Onion (essence).
  • Armory — equip, enhance, and synthesize gear for your Mons.
  • Inventory — view materials, shards, and items.
  • Daily Quest — complete daily, weekly, and campaign tasks for bonus rewards and milestone chests.
  • Trainer level — gain trainer XP from battles and quests to unlock stronger progression.

8. Rarity and skills

Every Monanimal has a rarity tier:

  • Common → Uncommon → Rare → Legendary → Mythic

Higher rarity means better stats and more skills. Hover skill icons in battle or the Wild Log to see what each skill does.

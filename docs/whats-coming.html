What's Coming

Planned features for MonEx. These are not live yet — gold and essence remain the main currencies until each update ships.

PLANNED

Arena — bet with $MONEX

The Arena statue in the Battle Hub will open as a spectator and betting zone for live arena matches.

How it will work

  1. Connect your wallet on the Monad network and link it to your MonEx account.
  2. Open the Arena from the Battle Hub when a match is scheduled or in progress.
  3. Watch trainers battle with their party Mons in arena-format fights.
  4. Place a $MONEX bet on who you think will win before the match locks.
  5. If your pick wins, you receive a payout from the betting pool. If not, your stake goes to the winners.

What you need to know

  • $MONEX is the Monad token used for Arena bets (and future shop purchases).
  • You pay gas in MON (Monad's native coin) when sending a bet transaction.
  • Minimum and maximum bet amounts will be shown in the Arena UI when it launches.
  • Only linked wallets tied to your logged-in X account can place bets — this keeps rewards tied to you.
  • Arena matches will use the same party Mons you train in Campaign and Patrol.

Status today: tapping Arena shows "coming soon." Betting, match scheduling, and wallet connect are still being built.

PLANNED

PVP Wager

Direct player-versus-player wagering — challenge another trainer and stake $MONEX on the outcome of your fight.

  • Find an opponent through the PVP Wager statue in the Battle Hub.
  • Both players agree on a wager amount before the battle starts.
  • The winner takes the pooled stake (minus any small arena fee, if applicable).

Status today: PVP Wager is not open yet. It will arrive after core Arena betting is stable.

PLANNED

Shop — buy Monballs with $MONEX

Monball packs in the Shop will support $MONEX payments on Monad. Each purchase is a one-time on-chain payment — no in-game $MONEX balance required.

  1. Connect your Monad wallet and link it once to your X account.
  2. Open Shop → Monball Pack → Buy with $MONEX.
  3. Approve and send the transaction from your wallet.
  4. Monballs are added to your save after the payment is confirmed.

Gold and KB's Onion prices stay available so free-to-play grinding remains a full path.
